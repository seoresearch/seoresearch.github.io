Prospective Postdoctoral Researchers

We are hiring! Please contact Hyowon with a cover letter, CV, and list of three professional references. Prospective scholars with training in Chemistry and Chemical Engineering and related fields are encouraged to submit applications. Those with training in (1) electrochemical engineering, (2) organic chemistry, including coordination complexes/organometallic synthesis, and (3) inorganic chemistry, including electrochemistry and photochemistry, are particularly encouraged to apply, although applications in other sub-specializations will also be considered.
