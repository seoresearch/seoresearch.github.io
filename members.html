Dr.Hyowon Seo (PI)

Dr.Hyowon Seo is an Assistant Professor of Materials Science and Chemical Engineering at the Stony Brook University. Her research is focused on electrochemical decarbonization engineering. She obtained her BS and MS degrees in Pharmacy at the Seoul National University, South Korea, where she studied synthesis of natural products with Prof. Young-Ger Suh. She earned her PhD in Chemistry from MIT, where she studied photoredox activation of CO2 in flow for the synthesis of value-added products with Prof. Timothy Jamison. While there, she received Korean Government Scholoarship and Amgen Graduate Fellowship. Before starting at the Stony Brook University, she was a postdoctoral associate in the T. Alan Hatton Group in Chemical Engineering at the MIT.

Education/Experience

2024-current: Assistant Professor in Materials Science and Chemical Engineering, Stony Brook University

2019-2024: Postdoctoral Associate in Chemical Engineering, MIT

2014-2019: Ph.D in Chemistry, MIT

2011-2013: M.S. in Pharmacy, Seoul National University, South Korea
