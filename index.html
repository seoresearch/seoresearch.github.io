I'm a 5th year PhD student in MIT EECS (expected graduation in Jun 2024), working in Quantum Photonics Group with Prof. Dirk Englund.

I am a research engineer with top-tier education training (B.S. in Tsinghua University EE, and M.S. & Ph.D. in MIT EECS) as well as world-class industrial experience (Apple Inc. product team summer intern). I have a solid physics and math background with the Asian Physics Olympiad gold medal and 1st prize in the American mathematical modeling contest. I can build end-to-end multiscale models using large-scale computations for the system from the architecture level to device detail with statistical quantitative analysis. I have fruitful hands-on experimental skills in electrical engineering and optical engineering for hardware optimization and failure root cause analysis.
